What I do

Three focused services. Each starts with a free 15-minute discovery call.

🗺️

Localization Production

Localization bolted on at the end costs twice as much and breaks things you didn't expect. I help studios build the right foundations early — terminology, workflows, tools, file formats, vendor relationships — so going global doesn't mean starting over.

What could this mean in practice:

  • Localization audit and budget planning
  • Pipeline design and vendor selection
  • Project kick-off and production support
  • Style guides, glossaries, and QA process

Typical engagement: 2–6 weeks

📋

PM & Workflow Consulting

Unclear processes, the wrong tools, or no structure at all. These are solvable problems. I assess what you have, identify what's slowing you down, and set up a methodology and toolstack that actually fits how your team works.

What could this mean in practice:

  • Workflow and toolstack audit
  • Methodology recommendation (Scrum, Kanban, hybrid)
  • Tool setup and team onboarding (Jira, Notion, ClickUp...)
  • KPIs, reporting templates, and ceremony structure

Typical engagement: 2–4 weeks

⚙️

AI & Automation

Repetitive tasks, manual reporting, scattered information: these are hours your team shouldn't be spending. I audit and map your workflows, identify what can be cut and automated, and help you build the pipelines that give that time back.

What could this mean in practice:

  • Automation audit and priority roadmap
  • n8n and Make pipeline builds
  • AI agent setup (research, summarization, backlog generation)
  • General or proprietary models

Typical engagement: 1–4 weeks

Most studios don't struggle at making games. They struggle at the infrastructure around them: the pipelines, the handoffs, the scope creep that quietly kills timelines, and the localization that gets bolted on a month before launch. That's the problem I solve.
