Indie Studio · Games · Apps

Turning bad ideas into joyful experiences

Bad Idea Interactive is an indie studio turning “bad ideas” into joyful arcade experiences with a twist... and learning apps.

Founded by an industry veteran, the studio blends classic game feel with modern production discipline: clear scope, tight feedback loops and a ruthless focus on player experience.

Every project is an excuse to explore new mechanics, teach something useful, or simply make people smile for a few minutes.
